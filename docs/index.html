How it works

It proposes. You approve.

Artist OS never turns you into a project manager. It watches the work and surfaces what needs you — you stay in the music.

01

Drop in your audio

Point it at a folder or drag in files. It reads your filenames, groups takes and mixes into songs, and stacks versions in order — v1 v2 final all collapse into one song automatically.

02

It flags the decisions that matter

Two competing hook takes? A newer mix challenging your master? Artist OS surfaces it in a Decide queue and lets you A/B them side by side, playhead held — then commits the keeper.

03

It hears your tracks

Every import is analyzed for tempo and key in the background, so your catalog is searchable and sortable by what the music actually is — not just what you named the file.

04

It keeps the record

Every version, every decision, every change is written to a running log — the creative history of each song, kept for you without a single note taken by hand.

05

Everywhere you work

Sync your catalog across devices with one tap and a code — no account, no password. Your audio stays local unless you choose to make a track available everywhere.

🔒

Local-first, by default

Your audio files and catalog live in your browser, on your device. Nothing is uploaded unless you explicitly turn on sync and choose to share a track. Your work is yours.
